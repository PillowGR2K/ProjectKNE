Rules for character creation:

Starting level: 1.

Roll 4d6 dice and drop lower. You are then allowed to -1/+1 once. If you roll 4 of the same number, get 20.

For each Intelligence (INT) modifier gain an extra Skill or Language.

Each player gains 1 free feat.

Non-Playable classes:

  • Artificer
  • Blood Hunter

Rules for combat:

(optional) Max dice damage for critical hits + roll normal dice.

(optional) System Shock mechanic.
